About Us

Welcome to Utility Base, a multifunctional tool website designed to simplify your everyday digital tasks. Our platform offers a variety of utilities, including Base64 encoding and decoding, URL encoding and decoding, JSON minification and beautification, URL shortening, and QR code generation.

Our Story

Utility Base is a project born out of our passion for technology and our desire to create something practical and beneficial for users worldwide. We are a team of three enthusiastic first-year students from BTU University:

Luka Chakvetadze

Nika Karseladze

Likuna Bolghashvili

As students of BTU (Business and Technology University) , we are always looking for ways to apply our learning in real-world scenarios. This project is a testament to our commitment to learning, innovation, and teamwork. We combined our skills and knowledge to develop a platform that addresses common digital needs in a user-friendly way.

Our Mission

Our mission was to provide a reliable, easy-to-use toolkit for everyone, from tech enthusiasts to professionals who need quick and efficient solutions for their everyday tasks. We aim to continually improve and expand our services to meet the evolving needs of our users. Our site is still far from perfect, but we are eager to continue working on it!
